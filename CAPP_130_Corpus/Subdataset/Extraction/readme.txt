
Extraction subdataset from 130 privacy policies of CAPP-130.

(1) important_identification_dataset
In this subset, important is classified as 1, and non-important is classified as 0.
(2) risk_identification_dataset
In this subset, risk is classified as 1, and non-risk is classified as 0.
(3) topic_identification_dataset
In this subset, the list is the one-hot vector of labels with id 0-10 in the Annotation's Label（Annotation/Label - Chinese-English Mapping.json）.
